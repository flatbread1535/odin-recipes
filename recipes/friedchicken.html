Fried Chicken

This is a delicious fried chicken recipe! It is very easy to prepare too. It should take only 25 minutes of prep time and 20 minutes of cook time. This recipe can serve up to 6 people. I hope you enjoy! :)

Ingredients

  • 30 saltine crackers
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons dry potato flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 cups vegetable frying oil
